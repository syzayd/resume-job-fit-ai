## Key Technical Constraints (Always Respect)

- **Free tier only** ‚Äî `gemini-2.5-flash-lite`; no paid services.
- **UI-agnostic core** ‚Äî all AI schemas + functions in `analyzer.py` (no Streamlit imports there).
- **Tests** ‚Äî mock `_generate` with `@patch("analyzer._generate")`, match pattern in `tests/test_analyzer.py`.
- **Auto-commit hooks** ‚Äî `git reset --soft HEAD~N` before every proper commit.
- **Input limits** ‚Äî 8000 chars per input; `_validate()` enforces this.
- **XSS** ‚Äî wrap all Gemini strings in `html.escape()` before `unsafe_allow_html=True`.
